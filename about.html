OUR MISSION STATEMENT

To empower young girls by amplifying their voces and fostering Leadership skills that enable them to become confident and competent leaders of tomorrow through education, mentorship programs and community engagement ensuring they become active agents of change in their lives and the community.

VISION

A world where every girl is heard, valued and empowered to pursue her dreams without barriers, we envision a society that celebrates the unique voices of girls, promote gender equality and creates opportunities for them to thrive, ultimately transforming communities and shaping a brighter future for all.

EXCLUSIVE SUMMARY

VOTGC is a non-profit initiative that aims at empowering young girls by amplifying their voices and contribute collective ideas from them and create an impact and pave them ways of being in a free environment of exercising their leadership skills so they become agents of change in their lives and the society. We envision a world where their voices are heard and valued, and create a society where their achievements are celebrated.

About Us

Aim and Objectives

Enhance Leadership Skills:

Provide workshops and training sessions to develop essential leadership skills among young girls.

Promote Education:

Advocate for access to quality education for girls ensuring they have the necessary resources to excel academically.

Encourage Mentorship:

Establish a program connecting young girls with female leaders in various fields to inspire and guide them.

Build Confidence:

Organize activities that encourage self expression and confidence-building, such as public speaking and debate competitions.

Fostering Community Engagement:

Create opportunities for girls to participate in community service projects, instilling a sense of responsibilities and leadership in their communities.

Create Network Support:

Facilitate the formation of peer support groups where girls can share experiences, challenges and solution.

Core Values

Advocacy:

Encouraging girls to take action and stand up for their rights and the rights of others, creating a ripple effect of positive change.

Inclusivity:

Embracing diversity and ensuring that every girl, regardless of background or circumstances, has the opportunity to be heard and valued.

Empowerment:

Fostering confidence and self-expression, enabling girls to articulate their thoghts, aspirations and experiences.
