Agau, founder of Voice of The Girl Child Initiative, is a remarkable young leader born in 2007. She founded the initiative in August 2024 at just 18 years old, driven by a passion to empower girls who lose hope without support. Having completed S.4 in March 2023 at Promised Land Secondary School in Juba-South Sudan, Agau believes girls can support themselves in many ways, aligning with her initiative's mission to build a strong community by amplifying the voices of the young girls.

Voice of The Girl Child Initiative aims to promote girls' education and rights through advocacy, education, and empowerment, focusing on preventing sexual and domestic violence.
